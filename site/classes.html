Make-up classes

Missed a class? Request a courtesy make-up in a comparable class — within one month of the absence, requested at least 48 hours ahead via the make-up form. Confirmation comes by email.

  • You must be currently enrolled; make-ups are non-transferable
  • Not every class is open for make-ups (size, age & level fit)
  • 90-minute classes use two make-ups unless you're enrolled in one
